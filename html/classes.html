Facilities

ML Strength offers a wide variety of group fitness classes for all levels, designed to help you build strength, improve endurance, stay flexible, and have fun.

Strength & Conditioning

The Strength & Conditioning classes are designed to build muscle, tone the body, and improve overall physical strength. Popular sessions like Body Pump use light to moderate weights with lots of repetition, while Power Hour and Sculpt & Combo focus on resistance and functional training. Classes such as ABT (Abs, Butt, Thighs) and Abs Blast target core areas to strengthen and define key muscle groups, making them perfect for those looking to sculpt and firm their physique.

Cardio & Endurance

Cardio & Endurance workouts are high-energy sessions aimed at boosting heart health, stamina, and fat burning. Options include Body Attack, a sports-inspired workout for total-body conditioning, and Body Step, which uses step platforms to drive cardio fitness. RPM and Cycle Extreme provide intense indoor cycling experiences, while Cardio Blitz, Cardio Circuit, and Hilo & Fat Burner offer varied routines focused on pushing your cardiovascular limits for maximum calorie burn.

Mind & Body

Mind & Body classes offer a calming and balanced approach to fitness, focusing on flexibility, posture, and inner wellness. Body Balance blends yoga, Pilates, and Tai Chi for a holistic workout. Yoga and Pilates are ideal for improving core strength and flexibility, while Tai Chi and Chi Ball emphasize slow, flowing movements to reduce stress and improve balance. Rejuvenate, Low Impact, and Gentle Fitness classes are perfect for beginners or those seeking a lighter, more restorative exercise experience.
