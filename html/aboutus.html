Welcome to ML Strength

Welcome to ML Strength – Australia’s freshest name in health and fitness. Based in Brisbane, we’re more than just a gym. We’re a performance-driven fitness community dedicated to helping everyday people train like athletes. With club locations across Ashgrove, Brisbane City, Chermside, Graceville, and Westlake, ML Strength offers cutting-edge facilities, world-class trainers, and a no-pressure environment that sets us apart from traditional gyms.
Our mission is simple: Fitness comes first. Whether your goal is to lose weight, build strength, improve nutrition, or just feel better, we’re here to guide and support you every step of the way. From group fitness classes to one-on-one personal training, we offer a wide range of dynamic training options including circuit training, boxing, strength and conditioning, karate, Pilates, yoga, and more. All our trainers are Les Mills-accredited and hold Bachelor Degrees in Human Movement, ensuring you get expert advice and results-driven programming. We’re proudly family-owned and operated, with a community-first approach. No sales consultants. No hard selling. Just passionate professionals and a loyal member base that speaks for itself.
What makes us truly unique? We’re open 24/7 – fully staffed at all hours. Whether it’s a late-night workout, a post-session protein shake, or simply a place to train on public holidays, ML Strength is always open and ready for you.



Our Philosophy

Founded by Maurice Lidman, a lifelong boxing enthusiast and coach, ML Strength is built on a foundation of discipline, strength, and passion. With over 30 years in the boxing scene, Maurice brings unmatched energy and authenticity to every session. From beginners to seasoned boxers, his fun and high-energy boxing classes offer one of the best cardio workouts available.
